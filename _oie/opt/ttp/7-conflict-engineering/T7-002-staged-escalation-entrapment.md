## Procedure
1. Secure absolute forensic proof of a local administrative failure or compliance breach.
2. Escalate this evidence strictly to the immediate supervisor of the failing node, explicitly avoiding direct submission to the executive board.
3. Allow the mid level executive to actively participate in the suppression or denial of the evidence before executing the final escalation to the chief operating officer.

## Goal
To infect the middle management layer with liability. If a local error is immediately sent to the executive board, the junior employee is simply terminated with zero systemic friction. By baiting a high privileged department director into defending the failure, you transform a local error into a massive departmental cover up, maximizing internal structural damage.

## Operational Logic
* Executives easily sacrifice junior employees to protect the overarching system.
* Executives cannot easily sacrifice entire departmental leadership teams without causing a systemic crisis and a complete fracture of internal trust.
* Engineering a conflict between the executive board and a highly privileged department head destroys the operational cohesion of the target entity.
