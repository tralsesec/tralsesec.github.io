## Description
The architecture of bureaucratic friction. By preventing nodes from communicating or de-conflicting, the adversary ensures that isolated departments remain in the dark, allowing the attacker to play different roles (like "Good Cop" or "Bad Cop") to different stakeholders without the risk of an internal information leak.
