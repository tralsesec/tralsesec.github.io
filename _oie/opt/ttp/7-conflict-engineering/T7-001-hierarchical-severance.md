## Procedure
1. Compile undeniable forensic evidence of localized administrative deception or failure.
2. Bypass the local management node entirely and deliver the evidence payload directly to overarching compliance or executive nodes.
3. Frame the local node actions not merely as an error against you, but as a deliberate rogue action exposing the overarching corporate entity to severe regulatory liability.

## Goal
To sever the operational trust between the local operating unit and corporate leadership. By forcing upper management to investigate their own subordinates, you convert an external conflict (You vs. Company) into a highly destructive internal conflict (Compliance vs. Local Management).

## Operational Logic
* Local managers rely on strict information asymmetry to shield their operational failures from upper management.
* Breaching that isolation layer forces corporate oversight to immediately intervene to protect the systemic liability.
* The local node is stripped of its authority and forced to defend its actions against its own employer, permanently destroying their capacity to resist your demands.
