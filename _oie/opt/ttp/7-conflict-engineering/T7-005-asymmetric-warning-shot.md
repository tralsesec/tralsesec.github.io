## Procedure
1. Identify a completely uncooperative or unresponsive mid-level node that believes they can simply wait you out.
2. Select one single, highly specific, and undeniable failure from their domain. Do not use your entire evidence dossier.
3. Escalate this single issue directly to an executive or compliance node, explicitly copying (CCing) the uncooperative mid-level node on the communication. 

## Goal
To break an operational blockade by demonstrating an absolute lack of fear regarding hierarchical escalation. By bypassing the node for a minor issue while keeping them in the loop, you force them into a panic state, compelling them to cooperate on the larger issues to prevent further escalations.

## Operational Logic
* Uncooperative nodes rely on the assumption that you are intimidated by the system and will eventually give up if they stonewall you.
* Firing a "warning shot" over their head shatters this assumption. It proves you know exactly who their boss is and that you are willing to use that channel.
* By only escalating *one minor* issue, you leave the threat of a full payload drop hanging over their head. The uncooperative node will suddenly become highly responsive to prevent you from sending the rest of your evidence up the chain.
