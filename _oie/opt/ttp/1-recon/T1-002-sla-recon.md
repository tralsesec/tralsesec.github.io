## Procedure
1. Identify all public facing legal and privacy documentation.
2. Extract response time promises from support pages and customer agreements.
3. Determine the escalation paths mentioned in public documentation.

## Goal
Determine the organizational tolerance for delay. If the company publicly promises a twenty four hour response time, twenty five hours becomes the point where the node feels administrative pain and panic.

## OSINT Sources
* Legal and Privacy Pages: Locate terms of service definitions.
* Support Portals: Analyze public facing help desks to define urgency.
* Job Postings: Search for roles like Compliance Specialist to identify monitored metrics.
