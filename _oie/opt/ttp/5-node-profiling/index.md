## Description
The adversary analyzes specific personnel and administrative nodes to identify individual psychological and structural vulnerabilities. This involves profiling targets for risk tolerance, tenure, and their personal stakes in the organization, allowing the attacker to identify who is most likely to suppress bad news or ignore issues to avoid conflict.
