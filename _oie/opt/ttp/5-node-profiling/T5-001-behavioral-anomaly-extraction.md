## Procedure
1. Deliver a high stress confrontation payload to multiple departmental nodes simultaneously.
2. Monitor the immediate automated and manual system responses generated by each individual node.
3. Isolate nodes that exhibit panic behavior, specifically looking for contradictory status updates or sudden manual system overrides.

## Goal
To identify the structurally weakest node within the target group. A node that fabricates a procedural excuse under pressure provides the exact forensic evidence required to compromise the credibility of the entire department.

## Operational Logic
* Coordinated pressure forces uncoordinated defensive responses from individual actors.
* Panic induces manual operational errors, which are permanently and accurately logged by enterprise infrastructure.
* Exposing the weakest node creates a localized compliance failure that can be directly weaponized against their supervising hierarchy.
